Our Purpose & Vision

**Grovance** is pioneering the transition to a circular, safe, and cost-effective student economy across India's college campuses.

  • **Risk Mitigation:** Offering a verified alternative to unregulated Facebook/WhatsApp groups.
  • **ESG Compliance:** Supporting university sustainability goals.
